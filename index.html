Our Heritage

A century-long journey of colour innovation and excellence

1923
The Beginning
Devarsons Industries founded in Ahmedabad as an application-oriented dye manufacturer, setting the foundation for a century of innovation.
1960s
Global Expansion
Began exporting to international markets, establishing partnerships with multinational corporations and earning recognition as a trusted global supplier.
1990s
R&D Excellence
Established formal R&D programs, developing proprietary molecules and customized solutions for highly specialized applications in medicine, NDT, and advanced industries.
2000s
ISO Certifications
Achieved ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and ISO 45001 certifications, demonstrating commitment to quality, environmental management, and occupational health & safety.
2010s
Digital Transformation
Fully integrated SAP platform across all operations, providing real-time transparency, traceability, and automated customer updates.
2025
Century of Innovation
Celebrating 102 years of colour excellence, serving 50+ countries, maintaining debt-free status, and continuing to pioneer application-driven colour solutions.
