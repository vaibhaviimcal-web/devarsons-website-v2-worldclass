A Brief Introduction

Devarsons is an ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and ISO 45001 accredited company, developing and producing specialty colorants. The group was established in 1923 with manufacturing activity based in Ahmedabad. With over 100 years of experience in Color Application, our product range is specifically designed to cater to customers in various industries such as Cosmetics, Crop Protection, Plastics, Petroleum Products and others.

Manufacturing Philosophy

The Devarsons Plant Ops. team has been assigned with the task of producing a consistent product within a very narrow spread and minimal deviation. This has been achieved with a combination of commissioning technologically advanced equipment, extensive use of statistical tools and other innovative approaches such as a formal purchasing policy ensuring consistency of the raw material stream. As an adjunct to our EHSMS Policy, Continuous Improvement is strongly encouraged to refine manufacturing processes and reduce carbon footprint by eliminating waste.

R&D Philosophy

Our lab is divided into 3 distinct sections â€“ Development, Analytical and Application each with their own mission statement.

The Development lab has two objectives. The first is to support the plant initiative of achieving a consistent product by way of efficient production methods. Existing production processes are constantly tuned to minimize waste. The second objective is to develop molecules as required by our customers and to actively participate with our customers right from the development stage of their products.

The Analytical Lab is fully equipped to test all parameters in-house which are required by our customers in compliance with global regulatory standards across multiple end uses.

The Applications Lab is committed to support the Company Philosophy of providing application based colour solutions to our customers. This includes identification of correct molecules for a given application or modification of characteristics of a known molecule to make it compatible with the proposed end use.

As a result of the long experience in developing and manufacturing dyes, our lab identifies parameters not forming part of specifications. These form part of an internal specification. Strict monitoring of these additional parameters, separates our dyes from the rest and helps our products achieve superior performance in the end application.
